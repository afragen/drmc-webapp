Dictation Mode Workflow
    1. Dial x6638
    2. Enter your Physician ID, press #
    3. Enter the location code, then press #
      • 1 for Hospital
      • 2 for El Mirador Surgical Center
      • 3 for Primary Care Clinic
      • 4 for ICON Ortho
      • 5 for ICON Neuro
    4. Enter Work Type (2-digits), press #
    5. Enter Account number (9-digits), press #
      OR, for EMSC (Loc 2), enter MRN# (6-digits), press #
