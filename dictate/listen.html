Listen to Previous Dictations
    1. Dial x6638
    2. Enter your Physician ID, press #
    3. Enter the location code for the desired dictation, then press #, i.e.,
      • 1# to LISTEN to a Hospital dictation
      • 2# to LISTEN to a EMSC dictation
      • 3# to LISTEN to a Primary Care Clinic dictation
      • 4# to LISTEN to an ICON Ortho dictation
      • 5# to LISTEN to an ICON Neuro dictation
    4. Press 4 to enter LISTEN MODE; you may choose the desired dictation by:
      • Confirmation Number: press 1, enter the confirmation number, press #
      • Medical Record Number: press 2, enter the 6-digit MRN, press #
      • Account Number: press 3, enter the 9-digit account number, press #
      • Work Type: press 4, end the 2-digit work type, press #
    5. To exit LISTEN MODE, just hang up; OR press * until you reach the end of the list, then press 2 to go to DICTATION MODE
  • Keypad - LISTEN
