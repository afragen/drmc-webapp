Edit Suspended Dictions
    1. Dial x6638
    2. Enter your Physician ID, press #
      (if you have suspended dictation, the system will advise you at this point)
    3. Enter the location code for the dictation you wish to edit, then press #, i.e.,
      • 1# to EDIT a suspended Hospital dictation
      • 2# to EDIT a suspended EMSC dictation
      • 3# to EDIT a suspended Primary Care Clinic dictation
      • 4# to EDIT a suspended ICON Ortho dictation
      • 5# to EDIT a suspended ICON Neuro dictation
    4. Press 6 to enter EDIT MODE; your suspended dictation(s) will begin playing from newest to oldest (if you have multiple unfinished dictations.)
      • To complete a dictation, press 2 and begin dictating
      • To close a dictation, press 5
      • To leave a current dictation suspended and listen to the next suspended dictation, press *
    5. To exit EDIT MODE, just hang up; OR press * until you reach the end of the list, then press 2 to go to DICTATION MODE.
